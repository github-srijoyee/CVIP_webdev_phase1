Get to know about 10 interesting facts of Indian culture

Indian culture is brimming with intriguing customs that reflect its diversity and heritage. From the respectful Namaste greeting to the vibrant celebrations of Diwali and Holi. Mehndi at weddings, the breaking of coconuts in religious ceremonies also play vital roles. Additionally, practices like yoga and Ayurveda promote holistic well-being, while the significance of the bindi and the reverence for elders emphasize cultural values. Indian cuisine, with its regional diversity, and the tradition of seeking blessings by touching elders' feet further enrich this colorful tapestry of customs, showcasing the depth and vibrancy of Indian culture.
